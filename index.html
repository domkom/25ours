25(h)ours

one hour is yours

  • Tweet
Problem
What if we had one extra hour? An hour to do the things we love to do. Those things we postpone because there are always more urgent things to do. Those things we would do if we had just more time.
Idea
Save a little time each hour and spent it at a stretch for exactly those things. Have a 25 hour day.
Solution
The 25 hour clock wrests 2.4 minutes per hour away in order to give you an extra hour at the end of the day. One hour is yours!
Result
Saving an hour a day results in 365 hours a year – which are 15.208333 days a year. That’s a nice vacation laying somewhere at the beach chilling, or hanging around with friends having a BBQ, reading a book, watching the clouds, dreaming away...
